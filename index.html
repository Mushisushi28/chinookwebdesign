FAQ

Common questions, straight answers.

What does "free demo" actually mean?

I build a working version of your website before you pay anything. If you like it, I'll send you a quote. If not, no hard feelings — you owe nothing.

How long does it take to get my site?

Most sites go from demo to live within 48 hours of approval. Complex builds with custom features may take a bit longer.

Do I own my website?

Yes, 100%. You own the design, the content, and the domain. If you ever want to move it somewhere else, it's yours to take.

What about hosting and domain?

I handle everything — hosting, domain setup, SSL certificate, and ongoing maintenance. It's all included so you don't have to think about it.

Can I make changes after launch?

Absolutely. Need to update your hours, add a photo, or change your prices? Just send me a message and I'll handle it.

I already have a website but it's bad. Can you help?

That's actually most of my clients. I'll build you a modern replacement and migrate everything over. Same domain, better site.
