Why bounded? Because fair to both sides.

My 14-day money-back covers the design phase. If you don't love your v1 mockup, you get every dollar back — no questions, no clawback fights.

Once you approve v1 and I move into the build phase, the refund window closes. That's the rule that keeps it sustainable: risk-free for you during design, fair to me during build. You've already gotten the design value, and the build hours are real hours I won't get back.

Approval of v1 = written sign-off via email or proposal e-sign. After that we're partners on shipping, not on rewinding.

Honest filter

This isn't for everyone — and that's the point.

I do my best work for a specific kind of client. If you see yourself below, I'm probably not the right fit, and I'll happily point you somewhere better.

  • Looking for the cheapest possible site? Wix or Squarespace beat me on price — start there.
  • Need a 200-page enterprise build? You need an agency, not me — I'll refer you out.
  • Want unlimited revisions for $500? Won't happen — that's how I'd burn out and underdeliver on you and the next ten clients.
  • Already have ads driving traffic and just need a redesign? I focus on businesses that don't have a real website yet — there are CRO-focused designers who'll serve you better.
  • Need a committee of five to approve every change? I work best with the decision-maker on the call — solo owners and small teams.
